Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the premium Stackly ecosystem.

How does the 24/7 professional monitoring work?

When an alarm is triggered, our professional monitoring center receives an alert immediately. A certified dispatcher will verify the emergency via video feed or voice hub call and dispatch local emergency services in seconds.

Can I install the equipment myself?

While our equipment is designed to look clean, we enforce certified professional installation to optimize camera viewing angles, secure sensors, and guarantee reliable network connectivity.

Is there a lifetime warranty on devices?

Yes. All hardware devices are covered by a full lifetime warranty as long as you maintain an active 24/7 professional monitoring subscription plan.
